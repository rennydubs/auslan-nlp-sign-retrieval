About Auslan Sign Retrieval System

Overview

The Auslan Sign Retrieval System is designed to help fitness coaches communicate effectively with Deaf and hard-of-hearing clients by translating common fitness instructions and conversations into Australian Sign Language (Auslan) signs.

Features
  • Multiple Matching Strategies:
    • Exact matching for direct word-to-sign correspondence
    • Synonym matching for alternative word forms
    • Semantic matching using AI for contextual understanding
  • Fitness-Focused Vocabulary: Specialized dictionary covering exercise, anatomy, and training terminology
  • Video Integration: Direct links to sign demonstration videos
  • Flexible Processing: Options for stop word removal, stemming, and semantic threshold adjustment
  • Real-time Evaluation: Performance metrics and coverage statistics
How It Works
1. Text Processing

Input text is cleaned, tokenized, and optionally processed with stemming and stop word removal.

2. Multi-Strategy Matching

Each word is matched using exact, synonym, and semantic approaches in order of confidence.

3. Sign Retrieval

Matched words are linked to their corresponding Auslan signs with video demonstrations.

4. Results Display

Results show match types, confidence levels, and comprehensive statistics.

Technical Details
  • Backend: Python Flask with NLTK for text processing
  • AI Model: SentenceTransformers (all-MiniLM-L6-v2) for semantic matching
  • Dictionary: JSON-based with 42+ fitness and general Auslan signs
  • Frontend: Bootstrap 5 with jQuery for responsive interface
  • Video Support: MP4 video integration for sign demonstrations
